# THEMIS
## Topological Heuristics for Ethica Model Inspection & Scoring

Student‑led initiative that investigates how AI systems can better account for historical injustice and structural bias instead of silently amplifying it. Drawing inspiration from Richard Rothstein’s fantastical "The Color of Law", the project targets the legacy of redlining—the historical, state-sponsored practice of denying mortgages and financial services to neighborhoods based on their racial composition.
